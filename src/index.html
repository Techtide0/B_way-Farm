Who Are We?

Welcome to B-Way Farm, your trusted source for fresh, high-quality farm produce delivered straight to your doorstep. At B-Way Farm, we take pride in offering a range of wholesome, farm-fresh products, including live catfish, frozen catfish, smoked catfish, ofada rice, and yams.

Our mission is simple: to make farm produce accessible, affordable, and convenient for everyone. Whether you're a home cook, a restaurant chef, or simply someone who values fresh food, B-Way Farm is dedicated to meeting your needs with quality products and a seamless shopping experience.

We believe that everyone should have access to nutritious, farm-fresh food without hassle.

That’s why our ordering process is straightforward—just add items to your cart and check out via WhatsApp for a quick, personal experience.

Thank you for choosing B-Way Farm as your go-to source for fresh farm produce. We look forward to serving you!
